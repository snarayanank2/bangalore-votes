## How do I find my polling booth?

Enter your address, the same way you'd look up your ward on the home page. This page returns your specific polling booth location with a map — not just a booth name or number, which is easy to get wrong when several booths share similar names in the same area.

You don't need to enter any voter ID or personal registration details to use this lookup, and nothing you enter here is stored against your identity — the same rule that applies to the ward finder applies here: no voter details are entered or stored on this platform.

## What if my address doesn't match a booth?

If the lookup can't place your address precisely, it degrades to a pincode-level result the same way the ward finder does, or points you to the official EC booth lookup so you're never left without an answer. If you're outside the GBA area, you'll get an explicit "not in the GBA area" message rather than an incorrect result.
