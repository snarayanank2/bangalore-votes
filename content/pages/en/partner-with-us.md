Getting this platform in front of every ward, and keeping every ward's data accurate, isn't something a small team can do alone. This page is how you get involved — no account needed to express interest either way.

## Spread awareness

Forward ward links to your network — your RWA, your building group, your neighbourhood WhatsApp groups. In return, you get:

- A **partner kit** page: ready-to-paste WhatsApp forward text in English and Kannada (including a first-time-voter variant), a poster you can share, and a short neutrality statement you can point to if anyone questions why you're forwarding an election link.
- A **tagged link** so your forwards are counted.
- A **report of what your forwarding achieved** — reach, not personal data about who clicked.

You don't need any special standing to do this — RWA office-bearers, building admins, and just-interested residents are all welcome.

## Curate data

Own the accuracy of your ward's data — candidate information, ward issues, and corrections. In return, you get:

- **Assigned scope** — your ward or zone, not the whole city.
- **Onboarding** on how to source and verify data properly.
- **Publish-immediately trust** — your edits go live without waiting for a second approval, because we vet curators up front rather than gatekeeping every edit.

## What's the expectation, either way?

Both paths carry a **neutrality expectation**: we're not asking you to promote a candidate or a party, only to help residents find accurate, ward-level information. Curators additionally go through a light vetting step before their scope is assigned, since publish-immediately trust has to be earned before it's given.

## How do I apply?

Fill in the form below — it covers both paths, and you can express interest in either or both. **No account is required** to apply; this is deliberately open to institutions like RWAs that don't map onto an individual citizen login. Submitting an application doesn't grant you access automatically — our team reviews every application before assigning a partner slug or curator scope.
